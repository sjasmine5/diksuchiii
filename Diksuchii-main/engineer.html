FAQ

Engineering is the application of scientific principles to design, build, and maintain structures, machines, and processes.

To become an engineer, you typically need to earn a degree in an engineering discipline and gain practical experience through internships or work placements.

Engineering has various fields, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical, Aerospace, and Software Engineering, among others.
