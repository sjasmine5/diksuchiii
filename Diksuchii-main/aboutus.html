ABOUT US

Welcome to Diksuchi

Diksuchi, meaning "compass," is a company dedicated to guiding students in 9th and 10th grades toward their ideal careers. We recognize the significant gap in adequate career counselling and guidance in schools, which often results in poor career choices among students. This misalignment can lead to mismatched skills, job dissatisfaction, and even unemployment.

In India, many students and their families are unaware of the diverse career opportunities available. As a result, career decisions are frequently made based on limited information or societal pressure. Diksuchi is here to address this issue by providing effective career counselling and guidance, empowering students to make informed decisions about their futures.

Our mission aligns with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, which emphasizes the importance of holistic education. We believe that guiding students in aligning their education with their career aspirations is essential for their long-term success and satisfaction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Diksuchi, meaning "compass," is a career guidance platform designed to help students in 9th and 10th grades explore and identify suitable career paths. We offer personalized career counselling, psychometric assessments, and resources to help students make informed career choices.

Our services are ideal for students in 9th and 10th grades, as well as their parents, educators, and schools. We help students explore career options that align with their strengths, interests, and values.

We use a combination of psychometric tests, personality assessments, and interest inventories to evaluate a student’s cognitive abilities, emotional intelligence, skills, and preferences. This comprehensive approach helps us recommend career paths that are well-suited to each individual.

Diksuchi integrates a holistic approach by combining traditional career counselling with modern assessments, such as emotional intelligence (EQ), social intelligence (SQ), and adversity quotient (AQ). We also align our services with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, ensuring a well-rounded educational and career guidance experience.

We rely on scientifically validated assessments and tools that are widely recognized in the field of career counselling. Our counsellors are trained professionals who interpret these results to provide personalized and accurate career guidance.

We encourage parents and educators to actively participate in the counselling process. We provide them with insights and resources to better understand their child's strengths and career aspirations, ensuring a supportive environment for informed decision-making.
