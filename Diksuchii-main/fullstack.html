Full Stack Web Development Mentor Intern

Be an early applicant

Key Responsibilities

Are you passionate about web development and mentoring others? DevTown is looking for a Full Stack Web Development Mentor intern to join our team! As a mentor, you will have the opportunity to guide and support aspiring web developers in honing their skills and mastering the latest technologies.

Selected intern's day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • Provide guidance and mentorship to individuals as they work on real-world projects
  • Offer support and feedback on coding techniques, best practices, and problem-solving strategies
  • Assist in creating and delivering workshops and training sessions on various web development topics
  • Collaborate with other mentors and team members to ensure a positive and enriching learning experience
  • Stay up-to-date on industry trends and technologies to provide relevant and valuable insights to mentees
  • Help foster a collaborative and inclusive learning environment for individuals from diverse backgrounds
  • Contribute to the growth and success of DevTown by helping mentees reach their full potential

Skill(s) required:

English Proficiency (Spoken) English Proficiency (Written) HTML & CSS MEAN/MERN Stack

Perks:

Certificate Letter of recommendation
